## Prime Earner Selection Criteria (24 domains)

Rebuild these with full Wayback content + monetization:

### Must Have (Score 85+)
- [ ] 4-6 character .com OR exact-match keyword
- [ ] 10+ Wayback snapshots with usable content
- [ ] DA 20+ or Majestic top 500K
- [ ] Clean history (no spam, no adult, no gambling unless niche)
- [ ] TLD is .com, .org, .net, or premium ccTLD (.io, .co, .ai)

### Ideal Characteristics
- Single dictionary word (gold.com, travel.com pattern)
- Two-word brandable (smartpets.com, homeguide.com)
- Exact match for high CPC keyword
- Previous site had active monetization (AdSense, affiliate links)
- Social media handles available

## Domain Naming Targets

### Highest Value Patterns
1. **Single word .com** - gold standard (rare at $10-15)
2. **Two-word exact match** - [keyword][keyword].com
3. **Verb + noun** - getfit.com, findpets.com
4. **[Niche] + guide/hub/pro** - travelguide.com
5. **Short brandable** - 5-6 letters, pronounceable

### Red Flags (Skip)
- Hyphens
- Numbers (unless brand like 3M)
- Misspellings
- Trademark conflicts
- Previous spam/adult history
- More than 15 characters
